The Problem

What?

Traditional Paper & Pen Voting systems are fraught with errors, expensive, prone to rigging, and inefficient. This has resulted in low level of trust and participation in most elections.

Who?

Educational institutions who elect student leaders, Public institutions who elect members, Private institutions that vote for leaders, Groups & Associations who vote for their executives.

Low Trust

The validity of an election system is based on the level of trust in the processes. Traditional election methods are opaque, insecure and do not make it easy to do an end to end auditing of the process for all parties to be satisfied with outcome. s

Low Participation

Voter turnout in most institutions have declined signficantly. The cumbersoome voting process and stree assoicated with casting votes discourages people from participating.

High Cost

The cost of printing, cards, ballot boxes, ballot papers, booths, security systems etc have raised the cost of traditional election methods. A lot of the cost items are not reusable.

Inefficient

Most election systems are designed to conduct ONLY one election at a time. This is an inefficient use of an expensive electoral system.

Accessibility

Existing voting systems are not disability friendly and inclusive for all vulnerable groups in society. .

Error Prone

Manual counting and transcription of votes introduce a lot of errors in the election process.

Why Now?

Increasing device & internet penetration

More than half of the world population are online. In Ghana it is estimated that there are more mobile devices that the population. With over 12 million ghanaians online, this is the best decade to launch an e-voting system

Covid-19 Pandemic

The covid-19 pandemic has accelerated the digital transformation agenda. Most people are now working, shoping  or schooling from home. For organizations that need to vote, online is the most appropriate option to adopt.

Access Levels

The system has five different access levels which describe the key players of the electoral process. These are the electoral commissioner, electoral commission members, election officers, voters and aspirants.

Electoral Commissioner

He is the overall administrator of the system. He sets up and manages elections and adds portfolios, aspirants, voters, electoral members and election officers.

Electoral Commission Members

These are people added by the electoral commissioner to help manage the election register and to help in the generation of secret codes for voters.

Election Officers

These users are added by the electoral commissioner to only have access to generation of secret codes and to only view the register.

Voters

They can be added by the electoral commissioner or the electoral commission members and they’ll have access to the public register, public ballot paper and the ballot paper to cast their votes within the stipulated duration.

Aspirants

These are the people to be voted for. They are added by the electoral commissioner only and they do not have any special access to the system.

Value Proposition

Transparency

With myThumb voting platform the whole voting process is auditable end to end. The design of the system allows administrators to guarantee users that their votes are correctly issued and accounted for according to the intention to vote. In addition, a voting receiptis issued for each user.

Increase participation

myThumb e-voting system maximize user participation, by allowing them to vote from anywhere and allowing access from different computer systems and from any device that has an internet connection.

Save on cost

The use of a single system for the entire voting process significantly reduces the organizational and implementation costs.

Accessibility

myThumb e-voting platform provides a safe and private channel that allows all users to participate on equal terms. Increased accessibility for voters abroad and for persons with difficulties in travelling or reduced mobility.

Precision

myThumb e-voting platform eliminates errors in manual count, transmission and tabulation which brings with it an accurate and quick publication of results, with receipt of vote for each vote cast.

Efficiency

myThumb e-voting platform can be used to conduct multiple elections concurrently. No need to reset systems to do next election.
